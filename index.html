Hi, I'm Jason!

Welcome to my website! I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the European University Institute in Florence, Italy.

I work on financial frictions, startup growth, and productivity implications. My broader research interests include time series interpolation methods, industrial organization, and political economy.

I provide research assistance to Alex Ludwig, and co-organize the Leo Kaas & Alex Ludwig Macroeconomics Working Group.
